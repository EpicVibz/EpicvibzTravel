Discover Comoros

A hidden Indian Ocean gem with volcanic islands and French-Arabic culture.

Best Time to Visit

May to November – Dry and pleasant.

Budget Overview

  • Luxury: $250+/day (resorts, private beach tours)
  • Family: $120–180/day (guesthouses, beaches)
  • Backpackers: $20–40/day (homestays, local ferries)
