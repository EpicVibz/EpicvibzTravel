Discover Kenya

Explore safaris in the Maasai Mara, beaches in Diani, and vibrant Nairobi culture.

Best Time to Visit

June to October – Best for wildlife viewing (dry season).

Budget Overview

  • Luxury: $400+/day (lodges, private game drives)
  • Family: $150–300/day (family resorts, national parks)
  • Backpackers: $30–50/day (hostels, matatus, local food)
