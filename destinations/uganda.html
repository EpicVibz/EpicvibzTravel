Discover Uganda

Home to gorilla trekking, the Nile River, and lush green landscapes.

Best Time to Visit

June–August & Dec–Feb – Best for gorilla tracking.

Budget Overview

  • Luxury: $350+/day (safari lodges, gorilla permits)
  • Family: $120–200/day (mid-range lodges, car hire)
  • Backpackers: $20–40/day (hostels, buses, chapati)
