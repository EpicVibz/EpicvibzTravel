Discover Rwanda

Known for gorilla safaris, clean cities, and scenic hills.

Best Time to Visit

June to September – Dry and perfect for treks.

Budget Overview

  • Luxury: $300+/day (Kigali hotels, permits, private drivers)
  • Family: $150–250/day (guesthouses, nature parks)
  • Backpackers: $25–40/day (hostels, buses)
