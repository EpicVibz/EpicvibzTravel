Discover Bali

Luxury spas, rice terraces, surf beaches, and Balinese culture await.

Best Time to Visit

May to September – dry season, perfect for exploring beaches and temples.

Budget Overview

  • Luxury: $300+/day (5-star resorts, private tours)
  • Family: $100–200/day (villas, family parks)
  • Backpackers: $25–50/day (hostels, scooters, street food)
