Discover Botswana

Untouched wilderness and high-end eco-safaris in the Okavango Delta.

Best Time to Visit

May to October – Dry and best for wildlife.

Budget Overview

  • Luxury: $600+/day (exclusive lodges)
  • Family: $200–350/day (safari tents, mobile safaris)
  • Backpackers: $40–60/day (basic campsites, bush travel)
