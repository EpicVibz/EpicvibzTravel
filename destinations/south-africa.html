Discover South Africa

Cape Town, the Garden Route, safaris, and Winelands.

Best Time to Visit

March–May & Sept–Nov – Mild weather, fewer tourists.

Budget Overview

  • Luxury: $300+/day (boutique hotels, private safaris)
  • Family: $150–250/day (car rentals, city tours)
  • Backpackers: $25–50/day (backpacker lodges, buses)
